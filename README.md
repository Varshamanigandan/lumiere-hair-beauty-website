# Lumière Hair & Beauty Studio — Website Redesign

## Task
Redesign a local service business website with a strong focus on lead generation and conversion optimization.

## Business
A hair and beauty studio offering hair styling, color, facials, and beauty treatments — designed to feel warm, premium, and trustworthy while making it effortless for visitors to book an appointment.

## Live Website
🔗 [View the live site](https://lumierehairbeauty.my.canva.site)

## Pages
- **Homepage** — Hero with clear value proposition and primary CTA, trust-building "About" section
- **Service Page** — Itemized hair and beauty treatments with transparent pricing and duration, testimonials
- **Contact / Booking Page** — Lead capture form with service checkboxes, weekly availability, and full contact details

## Tools Used
Canva (website builder)

## Key UX & Conversion Decisions
- Single, unmissable "Book now" CTA placed above the fold
- Transparent pricing to reduce booking friction
- Low-friction booking form using checkboxes and preset time slots instead of open text fields
- Trust-building elements (About Us, testimonials) placed before the ask to book
